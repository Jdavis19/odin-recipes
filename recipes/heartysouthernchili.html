Hearty Southern Chili


Description

Here is another quick meal that can be put together in under 45 minutes. Chili has always been a favorite of mine especially during the colder months. This is a very filling meal that is sure to satisfy the whole family.

For todayâ€™s chili we will be using just a few ingredients that you may or may not have around your home already.This chili pairs great with jiffy cornbread muffins.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef or Turkey
  • 12oz. can of Bush's Mild Chili Magic Beans
  • 8 oz. of sour cream
  • 12oz. cans of diced tomatoes
  • 12 oz. of Tomatoe paste
  • 12 oz. of shredded cheese

Steps

  1. heat a medium skillet stove top and place meat into skillet.
  2. Saute the onion and bell peppers in a skillet
  3. Brown the ground beef/turkey add spices of your choice, drain and place in cooking pot.
  4. Add can of mild chili magic beans
  5. Add can of diced tomatoes and tomatoe paste. Mix well.
  6. Let the pot simmer on low heat covered for 20mins., stir occationally
  7. Sprinkle shredded cheese into mixture or per serving
  8. Add sour cream for a smoother texture in your chili

This meal goes great with jiffy cornbread muffins!!!!
