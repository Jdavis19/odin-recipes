Mexican Casserole Recipe


Description

If Mexican food always sounds like a good idea in your house the way it does in ours, then this ultimate Mexican Casserole is a must for your meal plan this week! It combines pantry staples with fresh ingredients for that rich, cheesy Southwest sensation that we never seem to stop craving. Thanks to main ingredients that are often on hand at home brown rice, canned beans and tomatoes, and cheese this recipe is the pinnacle of easy and healthy comfort food.

For todayâ€™s Mexican casserole, I was hungry for something more old school and picked up inspiration from one of the most iconic, universally beloved Tex-Mex recipes of them all: 4 layer Casserole.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef or Turkey
  • 12oz. can of cream of mushroom
  • 8 oz. of sour cream
  • 12oz. cans of refried beans
  • 12 oz. of Shredded Cheese
  • 10-12 10in. Soft tortillas
  • 1 Onion & Bell pepper
  • taco seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahernheit
  2. Mix 2 cans of Cream of Mushroom and 16 oz. of sour cream evenly and place into your refridgerator
  3. Saute the onion and bell peppers in a skillet
  4. Brown the ground beef/turkey add spices of your choice, drain and return to the stove. (optional) Garlic, cilantro
  5. Add can of refried beans and taco seasoning. Mix well.
  6. Lay half of sour cream mixture along the bottom of a baking pan
  7. Tear tortillas to layer on top of the mixture to completely cover it
  8. Next, lay a thin layer of the meat mixture on top of the tortilla layer
  9. Lastly, spread a layer of shredded cheese over the meat mixture
  10. Repeat.
  11. Place in the oven for about 20-30mins or until cheese is bubbling
  12. Remove dish, and let it sit 15mins before serving
